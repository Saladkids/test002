At SaladKids, all tutors are verified and experienced, guiding children to read and speak with confidence, fostering skills through engaging lessons that inspire lifelong learning and joyful communication.


Flexible Schedule

At SaladKids, parents enjoy flexible scheduling, choosing convenient times that fit their busy lives. With personalized online lessons, children improve English confidently—anytime, anywhere—with expert tutors supporting every step of their learning journey.


Affortable

SaladKids offers affordable English lessons for everyone who wants to learn or improve English easily and confidently, with flexible scheduling, expert tutors, and personalized support every step of the way.


Private Learning

SaladKids private classes offer personalized, one-on-one lessons with expert tutors, tailored to each child’s needs and pace. This boosts confidence, accelerates learning, and ensures focused progress in speaking, reading, or grammar.


Group Learning

SaladKids’ group classes foster collaboration and communication by letting kids learn with peers in real-time. This encourages teamwork, confidence, and active speaking practice, enhancing their social and language skills effectively.


Mix Learning

SaladKids offers both online and offline learning, allowing kids to meet teachers live in person. This blended approach promotes personalized interaction, confidence, social skills, and deeper engagement, benefiting their overall language development.
